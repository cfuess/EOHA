Dr. Elinor Jordan

I am deeply committed to helping you be well so you can live your life to the fullest.

I work with patients to uncover the basis of their diseases. Together, we will investigate diet and lifestyle, the foundations of health, while also using herbs and supplements to ease symptoms. I enjoy working with patients who are ready to make changes in their lives to live in the best possible health. I employ several healing or investigate diets, such at the GAPS diet and the elimination diet.

I have been immersed in natural healing my entire life, starting with my mother treating the family with homeopathy. She taught us that symptoms are a guide to the cause. Just treating the symptoms may cover up the root of the illness. I love treating the whole family, whose wellbeing can be so intertwined. Children make up a large part of my practice. I regularly perform well child exams, treat acute illnesses as well as chronic pediatric conditions. Having a daughter of my own has taught me a lot about the parent’s side of being with a sick child.

I have successfully treated many cases of irritable bowel syndrome, inflammatory bowel disease, GERD or heartburn, high cholesterol, high blood pressure, auto immune disorders, thyroid disorders and many other chronic conditions.

My training: Bastyr University, naturopathic doctor degree, 2001 Evergreen State College, B.A., 1992 Sublingual Immunotherapy Treatment with the American Academy of Environmental Medicine in 2011.
